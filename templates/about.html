How to Care For Your Plants

  • Do some research about your plants
  • Make sure your houseplants get appropriate light
  • Maintain a regular watering and fertilizing schedule.
  • Check your plants regularly for signs of pests.
  • Clean and dust your leaves for healthier plants
  • Know your plants and if they're pet safe.
  • Trim any dead or dying leaves
